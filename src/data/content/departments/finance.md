
# Finance Department

The Finance department oversees financial planning, accounting, budgeting, and financial reporting to ensure the company's fiscal health and compliance.

## Key Responsibilities

- Financial planning and analysis
- Budgeting and forecasting
- Accounting and bookkeeping
- Financial reporting
- Cash flow management
- Investment management
- Risk assessment and management
